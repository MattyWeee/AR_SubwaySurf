How to Play Augmented Reality Subway Surfers:

1. Go to the Subway Surfers Online Game
2. Run AR_SubwaySurf.py  
3. Make sure your whole body is in view of the camera
4. Clap hands to set "standing" shoulder height
5. Clap hands to begin playing
6. Move left/right to move avatar left/right
7. Crouch/Jump to make avatar crouch/jump
8. Clap hands whenever prompted to "hit space"
9. Press 'esc' on keyboard to exit AR_SubwaySurf.py
